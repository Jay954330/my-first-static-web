Hulk: The Hulk comics follow the story of Bruce Banner, a scientist who transforms into a giant, green, and incredibly strong humanoid monster known as the Hulk whenever he gets angry or stressed. The series explores the inner struggle between Banner and the Hulk as they fight to control their shared body and protect the world from various threats, while also dealing with the consequences of their actions and the constant pursuit by those who seek to capture or exploit them. Overall, the Hulk comics are a thrilling and emotional journey through the mind and body of one of the most iconic and complex characters in the Marvel Universe.

Avengers: The Avengers comics follow a team of superheroes who band together to protect the world from various threats too great for any one hero to handle alone. The team consists of popular characters such as Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, Hawkeye, and many more, each with their own unique abilities and personalities. The series showcases their individual struggles and triumphs as they work together to save the world from villains, aliens, and other dangers, while also dealing with personal conflicts and the challenges of being part of a team. The Avengers comics are a thrilling and action-packed adventure, full of drama, humor, and epic battles that have captivated fans for decades.

Superman: Superman comics follow the story of Clark Kent, a superhero from the planet Krypton who possesses incredible powers such as super strength, speed, flight, and invulnerability. As Superman, he uses his abilities to protect the people of Metropolis and the world from various threats, including his archenemy, Lex Luthor, and other superpowered villains. Alongside his iconic costume and symbol, Superman's character is known for his strong moral compass and his dedication to justice and helping others, while also navigating his human identity as Clark Kent and the challenges that come with it. The Superman comics are a classic and beloved superhero story, full of thrilling action, heartfelt moments, and inspiring messages of hope and heroism.

Green lantern: Green Lantern comics follow the story of various individuals who are chosen by a powerful alien artifact called the Green Lantern Ring to become members of an intergalactic police force known as the Green Lantern Corps. Each Green Lantern wields immense power fueled by their willpower and imagination, allowing them to create anything they can imagine with the power of their ring. The series explores the adventures and struggles of different Green Lanterns, including the most famous one, Hal Jordan, as they battle against a wide range of enemies, both within and beyond their universe, to maintain peace and order across the cosmos. The Green Lantern comics are a thrilling and imaginative sci-fi adventure that showcases the limitless potential of the human (and alien) spirit and the power of imagination.
